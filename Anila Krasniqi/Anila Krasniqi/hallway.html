Hallway

Eclectic Style

Eclectic Style Entryway with Industrial Style Influence.Many people shy away from industrial style because it conjures images of something severe and cold. However, the styling in this space shows that the industrial style can be inviting and even cozy.

Mid-Century-Modern

A home entryway(hallway)is a great place to add an exciting and energetic style. In this space, the modsy designer used bold black and whites and clean Mid-Century-Modern shapes to create a dynamic space that will wake you up before your morning commute.

Traditional Entryway

This entryway has a pared-back style that lets it's individual components shine. The elegant arched mirrors and ornately detailed end table are so dynamic that they don't require extra frills.

Coastal Style

The home entryway is the perfect place to let the outdoors indoors. Here the Modsy designer has incorporated the seaside's colors and textures to play up the beachy vibes. This makes the space feel like a summer day at the coast and who wouldn't want to be welcomed home by that?

Organic Modernism

After a long day, it is so nice to come home and be greeted by a space you love. Take a little extra care to curate decor and art in your entryway design so you are welcomed home by things you love from the moment you step through the door.

Feminine Touch

Adding a feminine touch to your modern entry is easy by incorporating a few simple pieces. Try bringing in a colored glass table lamp to add whimsy and interest. Adding a mirror and console in a metallic finish helps to liven up a modern space. Also, be sure to include fun accessories like this gold lotus flower candle holder.

Black and White

Elevate your entry by using black and white accents to create a modern design. Adding in natural woods to contrast the cool-toned black and white creates a warm and welcoming space. Using black and white in the accents instead of key furniture pieces adds a subtle color scheme that can easily be changed or added to throughout the years.

Landscape

Create a warm welcome home for yourself with a cozy bench where you can kick off your shoes, and pair it with a lovely piece of wall art that inspires you. In this image, a serene landscape gives you a moment to decompress after a long day and leave your worries behind before entering the rest of the house.
